Overview

Music powered by friendship! Or by capacitive touch coupled with Bare Conductive Paint!, controlled by the Pro Trinket microcontroller / Audio FX Board

In this project, I drew two friends, a girl and a boy, reaching out toward each other, and reaching out toward you with their other hands. If you hold their hands for a few seconds, music begins playing: We're Going To Be Friends by The White Stripes. The Pro Trinket microcontroller is used for sensing capacitive touch on two input pins. The painting is drawn completely in Bare Conductive Paint, which conducts electricity, and the hands of the boy and girl are extended to cover these two pins, without touching one another. When both are touched for a few seconds, the microcontroller sends a signal to the audio board (Adafruit Audio FX Sound Board) to play the song, which has been saved onto the board's 2MB flash storage. The audio output is piped through a class D audio amplifier so that the sound is audible, which sends the music to a small speaker.
