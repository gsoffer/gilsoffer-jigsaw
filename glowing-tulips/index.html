Glowing Tulips

Overview

LEDs were embedded in this bouquet of tulips to produce a colorful glow!

The tulips used are "Real Touch" and have a realistic look and texture. An RGB NeoPixel LED was embedded in the bulb of each tulip, and a power cable extends out of the vase to the wall (AC power). The flowers slowly change colors together, and then take on different colors from one another, still changing colors slowly, but individually. The project continues looping through the tulips changing colors together and then separately. The four thin wires within a USB cable were used within the stems to connect to the bulbs, as four silicone coated wires were too thick to run through the stem. The thinner wires are also flexible enough to let the tulips droop naturally over the top of the vase.
