Overview

The "American Gothic" painting has eyes that follow your movement, Arduino Uno controlled

When someone walks by, the eyes of both the husband and wife in American Gothic follow. Motion is tracked using three passive infrared sensors. Each set of eyes is controlled by a servo motor, and a rack & pinion setup is used to translate rotational motion from the servos into linear motion for the eyes. The project is battery-powered, specifically so that you do not see wires coming out of the picture, which would give away it's hidden ability. The sensors are also well-hidden toward the back of a hollow stand.
